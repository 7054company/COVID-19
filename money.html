Financial Assistance

St. John Vianney Catholic Church

Provides financial assistance for electric, gas, and water bills. The agency can work with most providers, but help seekers should confirm with the agency. Provides bus passes.Provides vouchers for medical care to Spring Branch Community Health Center.

Note: Agency does not assist with utility deposits. Agency may be able to assist with late fees and reconnections on a case by case basis.

Program Hours: Mon, Wed, Fri 9:00 am - 12:00 pm

Disabilities Access: Location is accessible to people with disabilities

Eligibility: Open to anyone in the service area.

Application Process: Call to confirm agency can provide assistance with help-seeker's utility company and if so, set an appointment with the agency

Program Fees: None

Westheimer Career Office

Provides financial assistance for child support.

Program Hours: Monday through Friday 8:00 am - 5:00 pm

Disabilities Access: Location is accessible to people with disabilities

Eligibility: This is open to students or workers with at least 25 hours of occupation a week.

Application Process: Apply via email or fax through completing online forms.

Program Fees: Fees depend on personal situations.

West Houston Assistance Ministries

This location offers educational classes and exams, such as the GED (General Education Diploma).

Program Hours: Monday through Friday 10:00 am - 2:00 pm

Disabilities Access: Location is accessible to people with disabilities

Eligibility: Open to anyone.

Application Process: Call for further information, including application process.

Program Fees: None.

Harris County Center

Can provide educational and financial support as well as coaching. They also offer employment opportunities.

Program Hours: Monday through Friday 8:00 am - 5:00 pm

Disabilities Access: Location is accessible to people with disabilities

Eligibility: Must be stable with the ability to work a normal job. You must demonstrate a sufficient level of English.

Application Process: Call office.

Program Fees: None.
