Food Distribution Sites

NW Assistance Ministries Food Distribution Center

This location offers meals on each weekday from 8:00am to 3:00pm and is located in the Northwest Houston area.

St. Andrew Catholic Ch SVDP

This church supplies food to the general public on Tuesday nights from 5:00pm to 7:00pm. They also offer financial support for residents of certain areas. More information about that service can be found on their website or in person.

RCCG Pentecostal Life Assembly

Offers meals to the general public on Thursdays from 4:00pm to 6:00pm.

NRG Neighborhood Super Site Food Distribution Center

This site distributes meals each Friday from 2:00pm to 7:00pm. It is a part of Houston Independent School Dsitrict and the Houston Food Bank.
