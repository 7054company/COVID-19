Grocery Stores

Costco

CostcoGrocery Service powered by InstaCart. With Same-Day Delivery, Costco members can order perishable groceries online. The order minimum is $35. After they finish your grocery list, Costco will deliver your order!

Kroger

KrogerDelivery and Kroger Curbside Pickup allow customers to order online and have their produce either delivered to their homes or ready to be picked up at a designated time! Kroger's delivery fee ranges between $9-$11.

H-E-B

H-E-B Curbside & Grocery Delivery Service. H-E-B ships orders placed on heb.com to be sent all around Texas, to 46 states within the continental U.S. and to military bases worldwide. Has a $4.95 fee for pick up and $5 fee for delivery.

Walmart

Walmart Grocery Pickup Service. Like stores states above, many have Delivery and Curbside Pickup available. Stores are based on conveniance with regards to one's relative location.
