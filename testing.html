Testing Centers

CVS

CVS offers free tests to qualifying citizens over the age of 17. You must book an appointment in advance and bring your insurance card to the site. A federal program covers uninsured citizens. Test results return in 3-7 days.

Walgreens

Testing is available at most Walgreens. Begin by taking a quick survey and registering online. Tests are free if you meet certain criteria specified on the Walgreens website.

Amex Pharmacy

Amex Pharmacy is an independent pharmacy in Houston offering COVID-19 tests. They have drive-thru services with no appointment necessary.

CareNow Urgent Care- Memorial

This is another medical location providing COVID-19 tests. No appointment is necessary.
